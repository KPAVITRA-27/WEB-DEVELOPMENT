The Future of Amazon drone deliveries

Amazon has filed a patent for beehive -like towers that would serve as multi-level fulfillment centers for its delivery drones to tak off and land.The facilities would be built vertically to blend in with high rises in urban areas.Amazon envisions each city would have one .The patent application ,filed in december 2015 and published on thursday by the U.S patent and trademark office,features several drawings of these buildings ,such as the beehive,a cylinder -shaped center and one that looks like a UFO.
